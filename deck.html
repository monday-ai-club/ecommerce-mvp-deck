Risks & Threats

What could stop us —
and how we address it.

🚫 monday has zero brand equity in eCommerce today
Every account is a cold start in a market where we don't have credibility yet.
Mitigation: Start exclusively from monday's existing customer base. Year 1 beachhead is accounts already using monday — zero brand barrier, near-zero CAC.
High
⚔️ Salesforce Agentforce is moving to mid-market
Salesforce has the brand, the distribution, and an agentic platform. They're already in commerce.
Mitigation: They require full Salesforce stack + 12–18 month implementation. We deploy in days on existing monday accounts. Speed and simplicity are the moat.
High
🔗 Shopify API changes break integrations
Shopify changes its API surface or rate limits; connector breaks mid-pilot.
Mitigation: Platform-agnostic architecture from day 1. Shopify MCP is now a first-class interface — designed for stability. WooCommerce/BigCommerce as parallel integration tracks.
Medium
🤔 Design partners don't convert post-pilot
3 design partners use the agent for free but aren't willing to pay at the end of the pilot.
Mitigation: Define go/no-go criteria before pilot starts. Minimum 2 of 3 willing to pay = green light. Data decides — no subjective extension.
Medium
🤖 AI reasoning errors in the pilot
Agent fires false positives or wrong assignments — damages trust with design partners early.
Mitigation: Human-in-loop required for all actions in Phase 1. No autonomous execution. Agent surfaces and assigns; human confirms. Go metric: <10% false positive rate.
Low
Naming these risks builds trust. The biggest risk isn't technical — it's internal prioritization. Sunday is our window.
