The Problem

eCommerce teams lose revenue because critical issues are detected too late

Real Scenario
A hero SKU trends to stockout before a peak weekend. Campaigns keep driving traffic. The team notices Saturday morning. Result: lost revenue, wasted spend, and a reactive scramble.
This isn't a rare event. It's a weekly occurrence for mid-market eCommerce teams.

Why it keeps happening

  • Signals are fragmented across storefront, ads, and inventory tools
  • Ownership of issues is unclear — nobody's watching everything
  • Current tools surface data, not prioritized action
